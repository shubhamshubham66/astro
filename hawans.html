Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Hawan services

How does online Hawan work?▼

You book the Hawan online, we perform it at our sacred Hawan Kund, and you participate live via Zoom. You can watch the entire ritual, receive personalized Sankalp blessings, and get Prasad delivered to your home.

Can I join from abroad?▼

Yes! Our Hawans are accessible globally. We have devotees participating from USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and many other countries via Zoom.

How is Prasad delivered?▼

After the ritual, blessed Prasad is carefully packaged and delivered to your address within 3-5 days across India. International delivery takes 7-10 days.

How long does the ritual take?▼

Most Hawans take 45-90 minutes depending on the complexity of the ritual. You'll receive the exact timing when booking.

Can family members participate?▼

Yes! All family members can join the Zoom session and receive blessings together. We encourage family participation for maximum spiritual benefits.

How do I book a Hawan?▼

Simply click "Book Now" on any Hawan card, fill the booking form with your details, and our team will contact you on WhatsApp within 24 hours to confirm.

Are Zoom links shared on WhatsApp?▼

Yes! Once your booking is confirmed and payment is received, you'll get the Zoom link, ritual date, and time via WhatsApp 24-48 hours before the Hawan.
